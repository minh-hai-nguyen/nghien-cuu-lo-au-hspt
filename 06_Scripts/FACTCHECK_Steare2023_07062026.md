## 2. Scope & method (verified)

- **Design:** First systematic review về association academic pressure - adolescent mental health.
- **Outcomes:** depression, anxiety, mixed anxiety-depression (incl. psychosomatic symptoms), NSSH (non-suicidal self-harm), suicidal ideation, suicide attempts, suicide, mental health service use.
- **Databases:** MEDLINE (OVID, 1946-), PsycINFO (OVID, 1806-), ERIC (ProQuest, 1966-), Web of Science Core Collection (1900-). Search to **19/02/2021**, **update search 24/11/2022**.
- **Risk of bias:** Mixed Methods Appraisal Tool (MMAT).
- **Synthesis:** Narrative (KHÔNG pooled effect sizes — explicitly stated: "We did not conduct a meta-analysis due to the diversity of study designs and measures").
- **Eligibility:** English-language; children/adolescents in primary/secondary/sixth-form; mean sample age <18.

## 3. Results — số liệu chính (verified)

- **Studies included: n = 52** (24 via databases + 2 via review team + 7 via update search + 19 via citation searching).
- **48/52 studies** found evidence of positive association giữa academic pressure (or timing in school year) và ≥1 mental health outcome.
- **Breakdown outcome groups:** mixed anxiety+depressive symptoms n=20; depressive symptoms n=19.
- **Design breakdown:** cross-sectional n=39; repeated cross-sectional n=6; case-control n=2; prospective cohort n=3 (+1 examining exam-proximity); time-series n=9 (8 articles → 7 originals).
- **Geographic:** 26 Asia, 18 Europe, 5 North America, 1 Australia, 1 Europe+NA, 1 Europe+Australia.
- **Publication years:** 1991-2022; 40/52 from 2010 onwards.
- **Mean age range across studies:** 11.5 - 16.8 years.
- **School setting:** 33 secondary only; 3 both primary+secondary; 16 unspecified.

- Records identified from databases: **Medline 1,835; PsycINFO 4,440; ERIC 3,360; Web of Science 3,385** → screened after dedup: **9,898** (papers reported; flow shows n=8,198 records screened; n=9,794 excluded; n=106 sought; n=17 not retrieved; n=89 assessed; exclusions: no academic pressure measure 29, no MH outcome 6, population 3, study design 21, publication type 5).

**Lưu ý nội tại của PDF:** Phần 3 (text) ghi "13,020 records from database searches… 9,898 papers at title and abstract"; PRISMA box hiển thị 8,198 screened. Khác biệt này có thể do dedup + update-search merging — KHÔNG phải lỗi mình bịa; là điểm cần thận trọng nếu trích con số đầu vào.

## 5. Conclusions (verified, quote)

"We found evidence that academic pressure is a potential candidate for public health interventions which could prevent adolescent mental health problems. Large population-based cohort studies are needed to investigate whether academic pressure is a causal risk factor…"
